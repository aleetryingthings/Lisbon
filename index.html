Useful Information

Transportation Tips

  • Metro Red Line connects airport to downtown (25 minutes)
  • Tram 28 passes through many tourist areas including Alfama
  • Tram 15 goes to Belém district
  • Uber/Bolt are affordable and convenient, especially in evenings

Practical Tips

  • Wear comfortable shoes (hills and cobblestone streets)
  • Most restaurants open for dinner around 19:30-20:00
  • Tipping is not required but 5-10% is appreciated for good service
  • Lisboa Card: Consider for free public transportation (€44 for 72 hours)
