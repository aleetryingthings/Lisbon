Day 4: Sunday, March 30

Relaxed Final Day

Back to Overview

Morning

10:30

Brunch at The Mill

Why we recommend it:

  • Australian-style café with excellent brunch options
  • Bright, minimalist interior with Instagram-worthy presentation
  • Known for great coffee and healthy, delicious food
  • Popular with locals and visitors alike

12:00

Visit Gulbenkian Gardens

Highlights:

  • Beautiful, peaceful gardens perfect for a relaxed stroll
  • Lush greenery with water features and outdoor sculptures
  • Great place to unwind on your final day
  • Option to visit the Gulbenkian Museum if interested in art

Afternoon

14:00

Shopping in Príncipe Real neighborhood

Highlights:

  • Trendy neighborhood with unique boutiques and concept stores
  • LGBTQ+-friendly area with stylish shops and cafés
  • Great for finding unique souvenirs and gifts
  • Beautiful architecture and garden square

16:00

Coffee and pastries at Pastelaria Versailles

Why we recommend it:

  • Historic café with stunning Art Nouveau interior
  • Ornate mirrors, chandeliers, and marble counters
  • Traditional Portuguese pastries in an elegant setting
  • Perfect for a final taste of Lisbon's café culture

Evening (Departure at 20:30)

18:00

Early dinner at Tapisco

Why we recommend it:

  • Stylish restaurant combining Portuguese and Spanish tapas
  • Elegant interior with counter seating and open kitchen
  • Perfect for a final meal with various small plates to share
  • Located in the Príncipe Real neighborhood

Reservation recommended

19:30

Depart for airport

Allow approximately 30 minutes to reach the airport by taxi/Uber

20:30

Flight departure

Farewell to Lisbon!
