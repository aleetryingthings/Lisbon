Day 2: Friday, March 28

Exploring Central Lisbon

Back to Overview

Morning

9:30

Breakfast at Copenhagen Coffee Lab (Alfama location)

Why we recommend it:

  • Stylish Scandinavian-inspired café with excellent coffee and pastries
  • Beautiful interior with brick archways and minimalist design
  • Award-winning coffee shop (Best Coffee Shop in Portugal)
  • Perfect for Instagram photos

11:00

Explore Alfama neighborhood

Highlights:

  • Lisbon's oldest district with charming narrow streets and stunning viewpoints
  • Visit Miradouro de Santa Luzia for panoramic views
  • Wander through the picturesque streets at your own pace
  • Great photo opportunities with colorful buildings and traditional tiles

Tip: Wear comfortable shoes as this area is hilly with cobblestone streets

Lunch

13:30

Lunch at Zé da Mouraria

Why we recommend it:

  • Authentic Portuguese tasca in the historic Mouraria neighborhood
  • Known for generous portions of traditional dishes
  • Try the bacalhau (salt cod) or grilled meats
  • Local favorite with authentic atmosphere

Tip: This place can get busy, so arrive early

Afternoon

15:30

Visit Time Out Market Lisboa

Why we recommend it:

  • Massive food hall with the best of Lisbon's cuisine under one roof
  • Browse the market and enjoy a coffee or pastry
  • Great place to rest and people-watch
  • Perfect for food photography

17:00

Relax at your accommodation

Take some time to rest before the evening activities

Evening

20:00

Dinner at Dear Breakfast (for dinner service)

Why we recommend it:

  • Despite the name, they serve dinner too
  • Beautiful white interior with airy archways and plush pink and blue seating
  • Perfect for Instagram-worthy photos
  • Stylish, modern atmosphere

Reservation recommended

22:00

Fado experience at Mesa de Frades

Why we recommend it:

  • Traditional Portuguese music in a former chapel lined with azulejo tiles
  • Intimate setting for experiencing this UNESCO-recognized cultural heritage
  • Authentic cultural experience in a beautiful setting

Reservation recommended
