Day 3: Saturday, March 29

Belém & Relaxation

Back to Overview

Morning

10:00

Breakfast at Wish Slow Coffee House (LX Factory)

Why we recommend it:

  • Part coffee shop, part concept store in a trendy creative hub
  • Known for excellent baked goods
  • Relaxed, Instagram-worthy atmosphere
  • Located in the vibrant LX Factory area

11:00

Explore LX Factory

Highlights:

  • Former industrial complex turned creative center
  • Browse unique shops, street art, and the famous Ler Devagar bookstore
  • Trendy area with lots of photo opportunities
  • Popular with locals and visitors alike

Lunch

13:00

Lunch at Taberna da Rua das Flores

Why we recommend it:

  • Small, authentic restaurant with daily changing menu based on market finds
  • Cozy atmosphere with traditional Portuguese dishes with a modern twist
  • Popular with locals and food enthusiasts
  • Great value for the quality

Tip: This place doesn't take reservations and can get busy, so arrive early

Afternoon

15:00

Visit Belém (take Tram 15)

Highlights:

  • See the iconic Belém Tower (exterior)
  • Visit Jerónimos Monastery (if interested in going inside, otherwise just admire from outside)
  • Try the original Pastéis de Belém custard tarts at the famous bakery
  • Beautiful waterfront area with gardens and monuments

Tip: The line for pastries can be long, but moves quickly

17:30

Return to accommodation to rest

Take some time to relax before the evening activities

Evening

20:00

Dinner at Beco Cabaret Gourmet

Why we recommend it:

  • Hidden restaurant with dinner show by José Avillez
  • Tasting menu with theatrical presentation
  • LGBTQ+-friendly atmosphere
  • Unique dining experience in a stylish setting

Reservation essential

22:30

Drinks at Trumps or Finalmente Club

Why we recommend it:

  • Popular LGBTQ+ venues in Príncipe Real neighborhood
  • Lively atmosphere with music and dancing
  • Trumps is known for its dance floor and mixed crowd
  • Finalmente Club is famous for its late-night drag shows
